# English Learning Games

- **Quizlet Learn/Match**: Flashcard drills and timed matches to reinforce vocabulary; you can import custom word sets.
- **Baamboozle**: Interactive team quiz platform with plenty of shared ESL question banks; works well on shared screens.
- **Wordwall**: Templates like matching, wheel spins, and mazes where you plug in your target vocabulary or sentence patterns.
- **Blooket**: Arcade-style review games that reuse Quizlet sets or custom questions for competitive practice.
- **Kahoot!**: Fast-paced multiple-choice quizzes suited for listening comprehension and quick recall in groups.
- **Scrabble / Words With Friends**: Classic word-building games that strengthen spelling and vocabulary strategy.
- **Hangman**: Guess-the-word game ideal for reviewing spelling lists; easy to run offline or with simple slides.
- **Taboo / Heads Up!**: Descriptive speaking challenges that push learners to paraphrase without using restricted words.
- **Role-play Missions**: Scenario cards (ordering food, booking hotels) that prompt spontaneous dialogues and functional language use.
- **Story Cubes**: Dice-based storytelling that encourages creative narration and sentence variety.

Mix and match these games with your vocabulary or grammar targets, and add scoring or rewards to boost motivation.
